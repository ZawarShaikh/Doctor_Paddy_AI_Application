Our Mission

At Doctor Paddy, our mission is to revolutionize rice crop disease management by leveraging cutting-edge artificial intelligence.

We aim to provide farmers with an accurate, efficient, and user-friendly tool for real-time disease detection. By integrating advanced machine learning models, we empower farmers with timely insights to mitigate crop losses, improve agricultural sustainability, and ensure food security. Our commitment is to bridge the gap between technology and agriculture, making disease diagnosis accessible, reliable, and actionable for every farmer.

Our Goal

Our goal is to enhance rice production sustainability by reducing the impact of diseases on crop yield. We strive to achieve this by:

  • Developing a highly accurate AI-based detection system for rice diseases.
  • Providing farmers with real-time disease diagnosis through a simple web application.
  • Minimizing yield losses caused by undetected diseases at early growth stages.
  • Improving farming efficiency through data-driven decision-making.
  • Expanding our technology to support more crops and regions in the future.

Bacterial Leaf Blight: Causes yellowing and drying of leaves.

Brown Spot: Leads to brown lesions, affecting photosynthesis.

Rice Blast: A fungal disease that weakens plant stems and leaves.

Tungro Disease: Causes stunted growth and leaf discoloration.

Hispa: A pest that scrapes leaf tissue, reducing plant health.

Bacterial Panicle Blight: Leads to grain discoloration and yield loss.

By detecting these diseases early, we aim to provide farmers with the knowledge and tools to take timely action and prevent crop loss.
